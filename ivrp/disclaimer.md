## 1. Educational Purpose

The Inner Void Recovery Program (IVRP) is an educational and behavioral framework designed to support:

- Self-awareness  
- Personal accountability  
- Behavioral change  

It is not a substitute for professional care.

---

## 2. Not Medical or Clinical Advice

IVRP does not provide:

- Medical advice  
- Psychiatric treatment  
- Therapy or counseling services  

If you are experiencing:

- Severe mental health issues  
- Suicidal thoughts  
- Medical or psychiatric conditions  

You should seek help from a licensed professional immediately.

All users are responsible for:

- Their own decisions

IVRP provides tools and frameworks, but execution is the responsibility of the individual.

IVRP makes no guarantees regarding:
